Sparrow

The house sparrow, Passer domesticus, is one of the most familiar, widespread birds in the world. It is in many ways a remarkable bird, believed to be one of the oldest known bird species and a marvel of adaptation. Even if you aren't a birding enthusiast, you will quickly recognize this smallish bird

Crow

Crows are highly intelligent birds known for their problem-solving abilities. These sleek, black birds are often seen perched on telephone wires or scavenging for food in urban areas. Crows are social creatures and are often seen in large groups called murders.

Pegion

Pigeons, also known as rock doves, are medium-sized birds with a stout body and short legs.They are commonly found in urban areas worldwide, often roosting on buildings, ledges, and statues. Pigeons have a distinctive cooing call that can be heard echoing through city streets

Kingfisher

The kingfisher, with its vibrant plumage and remarkable hunting skills, is a sight to behold along the waterways of the world. These small to medium-sized birds are renowned for their stunning blue and orange feathers, which gleam brilliantly in the sunlight

Parrot

Parrots, with their vibrant plumage and charismatic personalities, are among the most recognizable and beloved birds in the world. These avian wonders belong to the Psittacidae family and are renowned for their exceptional intelligence, striking colors, and impressive vocal abilities.

Eagle

Eagles, with their majestic stature and formidable presence, are iconic birds of prey that inspire awe and admiration. Belonging to the Accipitridae family, eagles are known for their powerful build, keen eyesight, and soaring flight.

Heron

In India, herons are a familiar sight in various habitats, including wetlands, marshes, riversides, and even urban parks and gardens. These long-legged wading birds belong to the family Ardeidae and are represented by several species across the country. One of the most commonly observed heron species in India is the Indian Pond Heron
