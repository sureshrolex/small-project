TIGER

The tiger is the largest cat species and a member of the genus Panthera . It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange-brown fur with a lighter underside.In india tigers are very rare

LION

The lion, known as the king of the jungle.It's characterized by its majestic mane,muscular build, and powerful roar. Lions live in social groups called prides, led by a dominant male. They are apex predators, playing a crucial role in maintaining healthy ecosystems

FOX

Foxes are adaptable creatures, found in diverse environments like forests, grasslands, mountains, deserts, and even urban areas. They prefer areas with cover for hunting and denning, such as woodlands with brush or rocky outcrops.

ELEPHANT

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks. They use their trunks to pick up objects, trumpet warnings, greet other elephants, or suck up water for drinking or bathing, among other uses. Elephant tusks serve many purposes.

CROCODILE

crocodiles play crucial roles in their ecosystems as top predators, helping to regulate prey populations and maintain the health of aquatic ecosystems. Despite their fearsome reputation, crocodiles are fascinating and important creatures deserving of conservation efforts to ensure their long-term survival.
