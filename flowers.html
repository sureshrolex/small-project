Lotus

The lotus flower holds profound symbolism across various cultures, revered for its beauty, purity, and spiritual significance. Rising gracefully from muddy waters, the lotus symbolizes purity of body, speech, and mind, transcending the challenges of life to emerge untouched and pristine. Its ability to bloom radiantly amidst adversity serves as a metaphor for spiritual growth and enlightenment.

Roses

Roses, often referred to as the "Queen of Flowers," hold a special place in human culture, symbolizing love, beauty, and romance. With their lush petals and enchanting fragrance, roses have captivated hearts for centuries, inspiring poets, artists, and lovers alike.

Lilly

Lilies are stunning flowering plants belonging to the genus Lilium, which is part of the family Liliaceae. In addition to their ornamental value, lilies have some culinary and medicinal uses. Lily bulbs, known as "lily bulbs" or "lily roots," are edible and used in certain Asian cuisines. However, some species of lilies are toxic to pets, particularly cats, so caution should be exercised when planting them in households with pets.
